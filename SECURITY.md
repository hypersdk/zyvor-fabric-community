# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

**Do not open a public GitHub issue for security vulnerabilities.**

Email: **security@zyvor.dev**

Include: product name, description, steps to reproduce, potential impact, your contact.

We acknowledge within **48 hours** and aim to resolve critical issues within **7 days**.

## Safe Harbour

We will not pursue legal action against researchers who report privately, do not access customer data, and act in good faith.
